Name WILLEM
=================
Willem Goossens <willem.goossens@kuleuven.be> (sGLOBE lab - Division Forest, Nature and Landscape KU Leuven (University of Leuven) Belgium, PhD Student):

I am currently studying the role of alien plant species in pollination, with a particular focus on the potential benefits these species may provide to pollination networks that are increasingly stressed by global change. 

One of our objectives is to quantify pollinator visitation to these plant communities. To this end, I am keen to incorporate camera systems that allow monitoring at a relatively large spatial scale, ideally entire communities (1 by 1 m), although smaller subsections (e.g. 0.5 by 0.5 m) could also be feasible
